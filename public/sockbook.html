Sock Book

Why does it feel so wrong to throw out socks with holes in them? I didn't answer this question with Sock Book, but I did avoid it by keeping said old socks and stitching the lyrics to "I Want To Be Cold" by The Microphones into them. Sock Book is socks and a song I wore out. It is about time, double meanings, quality, and getting better--whatever that means!
