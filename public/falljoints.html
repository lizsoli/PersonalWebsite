Fall Joints

This project is very special to me. At the time, it was the first thing I'd made with my hands in months. It was also the first time I decided to make something without caring if the end result was "good," which meant it ended up being more vulnerable than I intended. At the time, it was the most liberating thing I could've done for myself. I LOVE fall!
