i was in norway

My friend Kiana lent me her digital camera. The first half of my trip to Norway, I recorded footage with little to no intention. But, a few days in, while listening to "Blutt" by Patrick Belaga, I had the idea for this video. It's about isolation, displacement, and solitude.
